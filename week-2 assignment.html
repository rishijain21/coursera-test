SEMICONDUCTOR

Conductors

If we have used up all the electrones avaiable and a band is still only half filled, the solid is said to be good conductor. the half filled band is known as the conduction band.

Insulators

An electrical insulator is a material in which the electron does not flow freely or the atom of the insulator have tightly bound electrons whose internal electric charges do not flow freely.

Semiconductors

A semiconductor material has an electrical conductivity value falling between that of a conductor, such as metallic copper, and an insulator, such as glass.
